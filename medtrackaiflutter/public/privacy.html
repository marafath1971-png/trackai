Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 28, 2026

MedAI ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your health information and personal data when you use the MedAI medicine tracking application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Profile Data: Name and basic demographics provided during onboarding to personalize your medication schedule.
  • Health Data: Medication names, dosages, frequencies, and adherence history (logs of taken/skipped doses).
  • Biometric Data: If enabled, we use device-level Biometric Authentication. We do not access or store your actual fingerprint or facial data; we only receive confirmation from the OS.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics via Google Firebase to improve App performance and stability.
  • Subscription Data: We use RevenueCat to manage premium memberships. No credit card information is stored by us; it is handled by the App Store or Google Play.

2. How We Use Information

We use your data solely to provide the medication tracking service, including:

  • Generating timely medication reminders.
  • Syncing data across your devices via encrypted cloud storage (Firestore).
  • Processing premium features through RevenueCat.
  • Analyzing anonymous crash reports to fix bugs (Crashlytics).

3. Data Sharing & Security

We do not sell your personal or health data to third parties. Data is shared only with trusted infrastructure providers (Google Firebase) strictly for the purpose of running the App. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

4. Your Choices

You can view, edit, or delete your health data directly within the App settings. You may also request complete account deletion by contacting our support team.
